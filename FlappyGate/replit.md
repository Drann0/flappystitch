## Custom Assets

### Custom Bird Sprite
To use your own bird image:
1. Place your bird image in `client/public/` folder as `custom-bird.png`
2. Recommended size: 40x40 to 60x60 pixels
3. Transparent background works best
4. The game will automatically use your custom bird if the file exists

### Background Music
To add custom background music:
1. Place your music file in `client/public/` as `game-music.mp3`
2. Supported formats: MP3, OGG, WAV
3. Music will loop during gameplay
4. Players can mute/unmute with the audio button
